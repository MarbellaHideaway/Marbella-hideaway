Welcome to Marbella Hideaway

A private Golden Mile villa made for unforgettable stays

Set in La Carolina on Marbella’s Golden Mile, Marbella Hideaway blends relaxed Andalusian style with the space and privacy larger groups need. Spend lazy days around the lagoon-style pool, gather on the terraces, or head out to the beach clubs, restaurants and nightlife nearby.

You’re around 30 minutes from Málaga Airport, a short walk to the beach, and minutes from Marbella Club, Puente Romano and Puerto Banús.

Space to gather

Six bedrooms, tropical gardens and a private pool

The villa sleeps up to 15 guests across six bedrooms, with generous indoor and outdoor spaces designed for social stays. From relaxed breakfasts on the terrace to private chef dinners and poolside afternoons, everything is set up to make group trips easy.
